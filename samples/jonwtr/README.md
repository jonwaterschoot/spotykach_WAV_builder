# Sample Pack by Jonwtr

A collection of noisy textures, field recordings, and voice sounds explored during the development of Spotykach.

## Contents
- **Textures**: Granular and distorted noise layers.
- **Field Recordings**: Captured environments and organic textures.
- **Voice**: Abstract vocal manipulations.
