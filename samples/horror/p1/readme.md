
# Synthux Horror Sample Pack 2025

![Audrey II Horror Sample Pack illustration](Audrey-II-Horror-Sample-Pack-2026-Synthux-Academy.png)

This Sample pack was made by members of the Synthux community, the draft for the invite to contribute was asking for samples made with Audrey II, Audrey Touch, or other gear in the spirit of the instruments horror-esque realm.

If you use any of these samples, please do get in touch with the makers and the community to let us know what you make with these. We'd love to see, hear and experience how these samples contribute to your work.

Find the contact details of each artist in the readme files. Tag @synthux.academy on Instagram, join our Discord community and post your work in the show-off channel.

Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/synthux.academy/

YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/c/SynthuxAcademy

Follow this link to join our Discord: https://www.synthux.academy/join

Go to our website to find out more about Audrey II, the horror scape synthesizer. https://www.synthux.academy/audrey-ii

### Text from the submission page:

> Horror Sample Pack 2025
Share your creepiest, most cinematic sounds made with Audrey II or any of your horror-inspired setups. Your recordings will help build a free community pack for artists and film composers. Please upload original material only and tag it so others can easily explore your work.
